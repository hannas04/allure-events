Who We Are

Every detail, held to one standard — allure.

Allure Nigeria Events is a full-service planning house for weddings, corporate functions and social celebrations. From the first proposal call to the final send-off, our team handles vendor sourcing, décor direction, logistics and day-of coordination so you experience the day — not the stress.

We plan proudly from Lagos, and travel across Ogun and Osun. For clients abroad, our partner desks in Canada, the United States and the United Kingdom keep the experience seamless from wherever you are.
